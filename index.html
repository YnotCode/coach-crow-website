Privacy Policy

The Coach Crow app does not store or collect any data inputted by users. However, all messages to Coach Crow are processed through the GPT-3.5 model using the OpenAI API as part of the advice generation process. This means data sent by users adheres to the privacy policy of OpenAI regarding its API, which can be found here: https://openai.com/enterprise-privacy
